Delete Your Maithri Account

This page explains how you can request deletion of your Maithri account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Maithri account, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at maithri104@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Delete My Maithri Account
  • Include your registered username, email address, or phone number used to create the account

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your request is verified, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Posts, reels, comments, and media uploads
  • Messages and chat history
  • Followers and following relationships
  • Activity and engagement data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes.

After this period, all remaining data will be permanently erased.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding account deletion or data handling, please contact us at maithri104@gmail.com.
